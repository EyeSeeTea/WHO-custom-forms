Co-blistered product: combination product packed in a single blister pack containing two or more different medicines
in separate blisters (adapted from 11). In this manual, co-blistered products are denoted by ‘+’, e.g. ‘artesunate + amodiaquine’.
Co-formulated product: product containing two or more APIs in the same dosage form (such as a tablet, capsule,suspension or injection).
Solid dosage forms that contain two active ingredients in separate layers are called bilayered tablets. In this manual, co-formulated APIs are denoted by '‘–’,' e.g. ‘artemether–lumefantrine’.
